Rentvine launched bulk bill approvals and configurable master approval thresholds to streamline the Money Out workflow, letting teams approve bills in bulk and set dollar-amount triggers for automatic approval or required secondary authorization. The feature adds role-based permissions so administrators can assign master approval rights to brokers, head accountants, or CPAs.
